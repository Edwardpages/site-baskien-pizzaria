About Us

At Baskien pizzaria we believe in the art of pizza making. Our dough is hand‑stretched, our sauces are simmered for hours, and our toppings are sourced from local farms. Whether you’re after a quick slice or a family feast, we’ve got you covered.

Come and experience the warmth of our wood‑fired ovens and the friendly atmosphere that makes every visit special.
